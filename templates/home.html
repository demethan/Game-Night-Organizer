Host Game Nights Without Group-Chat Chaos

Game Night Organizer helps hosts track RSVPs, seating, and updates from one link.

How It Works

1. Create an organizer account.

2. Create a game with date, time, location, and player count.

3. Share your invite link. Players tap the link and RSVP.

4. Track live status and optional seat assignments from your dashboard.

Do Players Need Accounts?

No. Players only need the invite link to respond and update their status.
